About Me



Rudy Rodiguez began his full stack programming journey with ideas to improve the efficiency of the small business owned by him and his wife. He came to the realization that there had to be a faster way to execute the many tasks associated with his workflow.

This led to the development of front and back end applications that he refined on the job and ultimate made available to his industry.

To explore some of Rudy's creations take a look at his portfolio which covers a diversity of subjects including websites, data entry systems, games, and more.

Contact Rudy to learn more about what he can do to help you develop the software you need to lead in your industry. See for yourself!
